Rustom Kerawalla

Rustom Kerawalla

Rustom Kerawalla, Founder Chairman of VIBGYOR Group of Schools, is driven by the purpose to put quality education within the reach of all, which will help young minds to grow and develop into progressive thinkers and leaders of tomorrow. With more than 29 years of experience, passion for creating the best and a strong focus towards a greater goal, Mr. Kerawalla has built one of the most renowned brands in the education sector - VIBGYOR Group of Schools - spread over 40 schools across India. Mr. Kerawalla is a well-known edupreneur and is regarded for his visionary leadership in the education sector. He is always acknowledged as a person who is a champion of “Good Thoughts, Good Words, Good Deeds”.

Kavita Sahay Kerawalla

“Nothing great in the world has ever been accomplished without passion.” — G. W. F. Hegel A trailblazer in educational innovation, Kavita Sahay Kerawalla, Vice Chairperson of VIBGYOR Group of Schools, has been an educationist for over two decades. She believes that education can be the catalyst to transform the world, inspire the young generation and make the world a better place. A powerful driving force behind VIBGYOR Group of Schools since its inception, an academician in her felt that the curriculum offered in a large number of schools did not equip children for the demands of the future. She led a team of experts who designed a curriculum for VIBGYOR that has a fine mix of global teaching practices and the Indian curriculum.
